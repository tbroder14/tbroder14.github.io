The Science Behind the App

Through a series of thoughtful questions and advaned algorithms, SARAH identifies where individuals are in their journye and delivers a customized wellness plan with practical coping strategies and tools that adapt to your journey. It's not about fixing or easing those feelings, but about providing the tools to manage them and find moments of peace.

SARAH also offers a support plan for a champion-someone close to the individual who can walk alongside them, offering guidance and care. This dual approach ensures no one has to face the struggle alone.
