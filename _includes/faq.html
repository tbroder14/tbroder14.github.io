Frequently Asked Questions

What is SARAH?

SARAH is a mental wellness app designed specifically for individuals living with suicide ideation. It provides personalized advice, support, and tools tailored to your unique mental state.

How does SARAH work?

SARAH uses advanced algorithms to assess your mental state and deliver personalized advice and tools that adapt to your journey. The app evolves with you, offering continuous support as your needs change.

Is SARAH a substitute for professional help?

SARAH is a supportive tool that complements professional mental health care. It is not a substitute for therapy or medical treatment but can be used alongside professional services.

Is my data safe with SARAH?

Yes, SARAH prioritizes your privacy and security. All personal data is encrypted and stored securely, ensuring your information remains confidential.

How can I get started with SARAH?

Download SARAH from the App Store or Google Play, and begin your journey towards healing and resilience today.
