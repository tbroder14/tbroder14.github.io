SARAH is more than just an app

It's your companion in mental wellness. Designed specifically for individuals living with suicide ideation, SARAH offers:

Personalized Assessments

SARAH carefully assesses your mental state using a proprietary algorithm that understands your unique needs.

Responsive Advice

Receive tailored advice and support that aligns with your current emotional and mental state.

Adaptive Tools

SARAH evolves with you, providing tools and resources that adapt to your changing journey.

Continuous Support

Whether you need immediate comfort or long-term strategies for coping, SARAH is always there.
